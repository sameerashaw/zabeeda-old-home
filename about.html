About Zabeeda's Old Home

For 20 years, we've provided care and comfort to the elderly on the West Coast of Demerara.

Who We Are

Zabeeda's Old Home has been a trusted home for the elderly since 2004. Our mission is to provide compassionate care in a homely environment, ensuring dignity and quality of life for all residents. We operate as a non-profit organization dedicated to serving the elderly community of West Coast Demerara. Our home provides not just shelter, but a true family atmosphere where residents can thrive in their vulnerable years with comfort, companionship, and comprehensive care.

Our History

Founded by Bibi Ulfat Mohamed, our home started with just five residents. Today, we house over 50 elderly individuals, supported by a dedicated team of caregivers and community donors. What began as a modest family initiative has grown into a cornerstone of elderly care in our region. Through two decades, we've expanded our facilities, implemented modern care practices while maintaining traditional values of respect and family bonds. Our growth reflects the trust placed in us by families across Region 3, and we remain committed to honoring that trust through loving care.
